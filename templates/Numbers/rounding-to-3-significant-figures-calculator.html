What is meant by rounding to 3 significant figures?

Before knowing about rounding off 3 significant figures, first we will know about significant figures.

According to experts, significant figures of a number is the digits that express with some degree of confidence. Rounding to 3 significant figures are almost similar to the rounding number of decimal places.

In this round to 3 significant figures as if we need to round to 5 digit number all we need to drop the last 2 digits and simply round off the remaining number.

How to Round to 3 Significant Figures?

In this rounding to 3 significant figures there are some rules to follow. By adhering those rules we can roundoff the 3 significant figures. Let us see what are they:

  • Primarily, we need to check the given number.
  • Then as this is round to 3 significant figures, starting from left leave the 3 digits towards right. And the fourth digit should be rounded off.
  • If the 4th digit is greater than or equal to 5 then we will round up the 3rd digit by 1 and simply remove all the digits after that number.
  • Otherwise, if the fourth digit is less than or equal to 4, then simply we remove all the digits after the 3rd digit.
  • That's simple, we will get the desired answer easily.
