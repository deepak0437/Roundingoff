What is Rounding Half Even Numbers?

Half even rounding is a rounding process that rounds half way values to the nearest even numbers. Round half to even is a tie-breaking rule that is even less biased. If the fraction y is 0.5, then q will be the nearest even integer.

The rules for rounding half even numbers are listed here:

  1. When the integer part of the number is an even number, then subtract 0.5 from the original number.
  2. If the integer part of the number is an odd number, then add 0.5 to to the original number to get the rounded value.

Detailed Steps to Round Half Even Numbers

Follow the below stes to get the rounding of a number to the nearest even number.

  • Observe the given number.
  • If the integer part of the number is an even number, then subtract 0.5 from the number.
  • If the integer part of the number is an odd number, then add 0.5 to the number.
  • Finally, obtained number is the rounded to the nearest even number..

The two basic rules for rounding when estimating is here. If the rounding digit is less than 5 i.e (0, 1, 2, 3, 4), then leave the rounding digit unchanged. If the rounding digit is more than 5 i.e (5, 6, 7, 8, 9), then add 1 to the remaining number.
