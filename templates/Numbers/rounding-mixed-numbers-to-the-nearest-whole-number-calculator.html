Steps to Round off Mixed Number to the Nearest Whole Number

Go through the easy step by step process to round a mixed fraction to the nearest whole number. Follow them to obtain the output whole number quickly.

  • Get any mixed fraction.
  • Convert the mixed number into an improper fraction of fraction.
  • Divide the numerator value by the denominator and note down the decimal number.
  • If the number to the right of the decimal point is equal to 0.5 or more, then add 1 to the integer.
  • If the number to the right of the decimal point is less than 5, then leave the integer as it is.
  • Removing the decimal point write the obtained number as nearest whole number.

Example

Question: What is 16(1/5) rounded to the nearest whole number?

Solution:

Given mixed fraction is 16(1/5)

Convert the mixed fraction to improper fraction

16(1/5) = ((16x5)+1)/5 = 81/5

81/5 = 16.20

The decimal part of the number 0.20 is less than 0.5, then leave the interger part as it is.

i.e 16.20 = 16

Therefore, 16(1/5) rounded to the nearest whole number is is 16.

FAQ's on Rounding Fractions Calculator

1. How do you round a mixed number to the nearest whole number?

You need to convert the mixed number into a fraction. Divide the numerator by the denominator of the fraction to get the decimal number. Using the rounding rules, round the obtained decimal to the nearest whole number.

2. How do I round a fraction?

Make use of the free rounding fractions calculator to round off a fraction to the nearest half or nearest whole number or nearest tenths and so on. Just enter the fraction as input and press the calculate button to obtain the result easily.

3. What is a mixed number?

A mixed number is a combination of a fraction and a whole number. It is a kind of fraction. The example of a mixed fraction is 1(2/3).

4. Find the nearest rounded value of 3(3/4)?

Given mixed number is 3(3/4)

Convert it into a fraction.

3(3/4) = ((3*4))+3)/4 = 15/4

15/4 = 3.75

As per the rounding rules, the number to the right of the decimal point is greater than 5 add 1 to the left of the decimal point.

i.e 3+1 = 4

Therefore, 3(3/4) is rounded to the nearest whole number is 4.
