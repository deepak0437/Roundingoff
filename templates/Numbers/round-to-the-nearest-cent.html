How to Round to the Nearest Cent?

Follow the simple instructions to round the value to its nearest cent. By adhering to these instructions you can arrive at the solution easily. They are outlined as such

  • To round the amount to the nearest cent check the value of its thousandths place.
  • If the value is greater than or equal to 5 increase the cent's place in hundredths by 1 and remove all the digits right after the second decimal value.
  • If the value is less than 5 or less remove all digits right next to the second decimal value.

Example

Question : If the Amount is 5.3227 round it to the Nearest Cent?

Solution:

Given Amount = 5.3227

Firstly, check for the thousandths place

In this case, thousandths place is 2 as it is less than 5 you can remove the values next to it.

Thus 5.3227 becomes 5.32

Therefore, 5.3227 rounded to nearest cent is 5.32
