How to Round to the Nearest Hundred Millionths? (Step-by-Step)

The following are the simple steps or certain rules to round off the decimals to the nearest 100000000th.

  • The initial step is to identify the hundred millionth place value and right to it which is the billionth place value digit to round off the number.
  • Next, you need to take the billionth place digit and check with the rounding rules.
  • If the rounding digit is 0,1,2,3,4, then round down the number. Keep the same digit in 100000000th place and remove all the digits after the rounding value.
  • If the rounding digit is 5,6,7,8,9, then round up the number. Add one to the rounding value and remove all the digits after it.
  • At last, you will get the required rounded value to the nearest hundred millionth place.
