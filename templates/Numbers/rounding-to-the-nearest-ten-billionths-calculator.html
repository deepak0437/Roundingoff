Detailed Steps to Round Off Decimal to the Nearest 10000000000th

Rounding to the Nearest Ten Billionths is the process of trimming the decimal number to the closest place value. In decimals, ten billionths place is the 10th position to the right of the decimal point. The simple steps to round off the decimal to the nearest 1/10000000000 are listed below. Follow them carefully while rounding decimals.

  • First and foremost, look at the given number.
  • Then, recognize the digits in ten billionths and hundred billionths unit places.
  • To round off decimals to the nearest ten billionth, we have to look for the next digit which is a hundred billionths digit.
  • If it is greater than or equal to 5, then we will round up.
  • If it is less than 5, then we will round down.
