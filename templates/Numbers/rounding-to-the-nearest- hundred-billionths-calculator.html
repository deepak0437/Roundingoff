How to Round to the Nearest Hundred Billionths?

Below are the steps to round to the nearest hundred billionths. Follow these steps and get the output easily and quickly.

  • First, you need to mark the value in hundred billionths place.
  • Then look for the thousand billionths value which is available right to the hundred billionths place.
  • If the thousand billionths value is greater than or equal to 5 then the hundred billionths place digit increases by 1 and the digits right to it becomes zero.
  • If the value is 4 or below then remove all the digits after the hundred billionths place.
  • Finally, you will get the desired answer.
