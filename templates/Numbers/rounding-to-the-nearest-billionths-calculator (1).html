What is Meant by Rounding Decimals to Nearest Billionths?

Rounding Decimals to the Nearest Billionths is the method of trimming the given number to its nearest place value. Check what are the rules and steps to round off the decimals to nearest 1000000000th from our page and learn well.

For Example, the decimal number is 17.8924357812. Now, the rounded value to its nearest billionth is 17.892435781.

Rules to Round Decimal to the Nearest Billionth

Take a look at the rounding decimal rules and a step-by-step process for rounding the numbers to the nearest billionths from below.

  • At first, identify the given number and find the billionth and ten billionths place.
  • If the ten billionth digit is less than 5, then we will round down.
  • If the ten billionth digit is more than or equal to 5, then we will round up.

Find the rounding to the nearest billionth for the decimal: 5.8702567241

Solution:
