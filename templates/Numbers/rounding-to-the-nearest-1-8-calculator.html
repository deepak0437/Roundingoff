What is Rounding Numbers to Nearest One-Eighth?

Rounding to the nearest of ⅛ is the process of trimming down or up the number that is nearest to one eighth. Here, you can check the closest multiple of fraction ⅛ for any decimal number.

Example: Rounding of 100.245 to the nearest 1/8 is 100 2/8.

Step by Step Process to Round Off to the Nearest ⅛

Here are the simple steps that you should remember when rounding off numbers to the nearest 1/8 manually:

  • First, get the number.
  • Identify the fractional part for the given number.
  • Find the nearest value that is equivalent to the multiple of ⅛
  • Represent the value in the form of fraction.
  • If the given number is near to the next number, then round up the number.

The steps to round off numbers are here.

  3. If it is 5 or more, then add 1 to it and trim the values right of it.
