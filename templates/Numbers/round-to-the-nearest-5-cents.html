Procedure to Round to the Nearest 5 Cents

If you are looking for help on how to round to the nearest 5 cents this is the right place. Check out the instructions listed below and round up or down to the nearest 5 cents. They are outlined as below

  • The first and foremost step is to check the nearest cent value.
  • If it is 0 or 5 do nothing.
  • If it is 3, 4, 8, 9 round up to the nearest 5 cents or else round down to the nearest 5 cents.
