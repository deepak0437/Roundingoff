What are Kilometer and Steps?

Kilometer:

In the metric system, a kilometer is the unit of length. It means one kilometer is approximately equal to 1000 meters. And the SI unit of these kilometers is “km”.

Steps:

In roman units, a step is also a unit of length. One step is approximately equal to 0.762 meters or 2.5 feet as per standardization.

Formula For Calculating Kilometers To Steps

Here we will look into the formula to calculate the kilometer to steps easily.

Steps = km x conversion factor value

Where,

km = kilometers

Conversion factor value = 1312.33595801.
