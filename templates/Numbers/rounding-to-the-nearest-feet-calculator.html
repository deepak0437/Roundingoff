What is Meant by Round Off to Nearest Feet (ft)?

Trimming the input number to the closest rounding value is known as rounding off to the nearest feet. The process to find the round off value to the nearest foot is explained very clearly in a step-wise manner below.

How to Round to the Nearest Feet (Step-by-Step)?

Take a look at the simple procedure of rounding to the nearest feet in the provided points and get familiar with the calculation manually:

  • First and foremost, identify the given feet number.
  • Now, look at the digit that we have to round off which is the first digit after the decimal point.
  • If that value is between 0 to 4, then leave the digit we care for and trim all digits to the right.
  • If the value is between 5 to 9, then increment the number we care about and trim all the right of it.
  • Finally, the left out number is the rounded value to the nearest ft.

Example:

What is 23.879 ft to the nearest feet?

Solution:

Given input is 23.879 ft

The digit at the tenth place is 8 which is greater than 5

So, round up the number

Hence, 23.879 ft rounded to the nearest feet is 24 ft.
