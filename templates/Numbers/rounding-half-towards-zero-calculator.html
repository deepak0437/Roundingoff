What Does Meant by Rounding Half Towards Zero?

Rounding Half Towards Zero is a symmetric rounding technique that rounds the half numbers to the nearest zero. The positive half numbers are rounded up to get the closer to zero. In case of negative numbers, the numbers are rounded down to get closer to zero.

Example: -8.5 is rounded towards zero is -9.

How to Round Numbers Towards Zero?

Have a look at the simple steps to round half numbers to the nearest zero easily. Go through these steps to find the answer easily.

  • At first, you need to check the number.
  • If it is positive and its decimal part is greater than or equal to 5, then round up the number.
  • If it is negative and its decimal part is less than 5, then round down the number.
  • When the given number is negative and its decimal part is below 5, then round up the number.
  • Otherwise, round down the number.

FAQs on Rounding Half To Zero Calculator

1. What is meant by rounding?

Rounding a number means making it simpler by keeping its original value intact but closer to the next number. Rounding off numbers is done to preserve the significant figures.

2. What is round half away from zero?

Rounding half away from zero is a tie-breaking rounding method and it is used to round numbers half away from zero. The positive number which has 0.5 are rounded up while the negative numbers are rounded down.

3. How do you round the number towards zero?

To round a number towards zero, we have to follow these rules. The numbers which have digits from 0 to 4 as rounding digits are rounded down. And the numbers that have rounding digits as 5 to 9 are rounded up.

4. What is 56.5 rounded towards zero?

56.5 rounded towards zero is 57.
