How to Round to the Nearest 10 Cents?

Here are some guidelines to round off the amount to the nearest 10 cents. Go through these instructions to solve the problems on your own.

  • Primarily, we need to check the nearest cent value.
  • If it is 0 you do nothing.
  • If it is 1, 2, 3, 4 round down to the nearest 10 cents and change the second digit next to the decimal value to 0.
  • If it is 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 round up to the nearest 10 cents and change the second digit right after the decimal value to 0 and increase the one's place value of the amount by 1.
