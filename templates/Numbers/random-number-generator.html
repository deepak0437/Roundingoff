How to Generate Random Numbers?

Learn all about Random Numbers and how to find them manually from here. 

A Random Number is a number chosen from a pool of limited or unlimited numbers that have no pattern. The number pool is always independent of each other. However, at times they may follow a specific distribution. You can follow any method of Randomization that you are comfortable with to randomize numbers.
