Procedure to Round Off the Decimal to Nearest Ten Quadrillionth

For rounding the decimal to the nearest ten quadrillionths follow the steps given below.

  • Looking for the ten quadrillionth value in the given problem.
  • Then search for the next digit value right to the rounding value.
  • Hundred quadrillionths place is above or = 5 then round up and add +1.
  • Or, the hundred quadrillionths value is less than 5 then round down.
  • After that simply remove all the digits right to the ten quadrillionths place.
