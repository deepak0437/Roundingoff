FAQ's on Time Clock 15 Minutes Rounding Calculator

1. How do you calculate 15-minute increments?

If you want to round to 15 minute increments, observe the 7-minute rule, for every 1 to 7 minutes that are rounded down, there is a corresponding timeframe of 8 to 14 minutes that are rounded up.

2. What is 15 minutes on a time clock?

15 minutes on a time clock is called one quater hour or 0.25 hour.

3. What Is Time Clock Rounding?

Time clock rounding is a practice that allows supervisors to get an insight on their employee's work hours and modify them to easier to tackle numbers for accounting when they calculate payroll.
