What Does Meant by Round off to the Nearest 1⁄16?

Rounding to the nearest one by sixteen means estimating the and representing the frcational part of the decimal numbers in the fraction of 16.

Example: Rounding of 112.245 to the nearest 1/16 is 112 4⁄16.

Rules to Round Numbers to Nearest 1⁄16

Go through the steps and rules to round off decimal numbers to the nearest 1⁄16 quickly and effortlessly.

  • Get the decimal numbers that should be rounded.
  • Observe the fractional part of the number.
  • Round the fractional part to the number that can be represented in fraction of 16.
  • Write the rounded number as a fraction that has 16 as the denominator.
  • Finally, give the integer part and fraction as rounded value.
