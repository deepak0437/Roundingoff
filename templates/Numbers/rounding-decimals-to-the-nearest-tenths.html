How to Round Decimals to the Nearest Tenths?

Follow the rules to keep in mind while rounding decimals to the nearest tenths. They are in the following fashion

  • To round a decimal nearest to tenths analyze the digit in the hundredths place.
  • If the hundredths place value is >=5 then the digit at the tenths place increases by 1 and the digits at hundredths place or thereafter becomes zero.
  • If the hundredths place is 4 or less than that simply remove all the digits right of the hundredths place along with the hundredths place digit.

Example

Question : Round 17. 273 to the nearest tenths?

Solution:

Given Decimal Value is 17.273

Analyze the hundredths place digit i.e. 7

As it is greater than 5 increase the tenths place by 1 and remove all the digits in the hundredths place and thereafter them.

= 17.3

Thus, 17.273 rounded to the nearest tenths is 17.3
