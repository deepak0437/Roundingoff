How to Round to the Nearest Integer?

Go through the instructions below to round to the nearest integer and solve related problems on your own. They are along the lines

  • To round a decimal number to the nearest integer firstly examine the value of its first digit.
  • If the value is greater than or equal to 5 increase the value in the integers ones place by 1 and remove all the digits.
  • If the value is less than or equal to 4 simply remove all the digits and the decimal separator.

Example

Question : Round 2.256, 99.1 to the Nearest Integer?

Solution:

Given Data 2.256, 99.1

2.256 - The first digits value is 2 so we round down and it becomes 2

99.1 - The first digit value is 1 so we round down and it becomes 99
