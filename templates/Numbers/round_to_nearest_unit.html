Rounding to Nearest Unit Definition

Rounding to the Nearest Unit is nothing but the process of approximating to the nearest integer and cutting down the decimal part if any. For Example: 45.768 rounded to the nearest unit is 46.

How to Approximate or Round to the Nearest Unit?

In order to round to the nearest unit you need to keep certain rules in mind. They are explained in detaled steps below

  • First step to keep in mind is to look at the digit written in tenths place.
  • If the digit in the tenths place is >=5 simply increase the units digit by 1 and cut down the decimal part if any.
  • If the digit in the tenths place is <=4 simply cut down the decimal part and keep the units place same.
