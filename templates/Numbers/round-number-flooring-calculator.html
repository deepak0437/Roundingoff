What is Meant by Rounding Number Flooring?

Rounding Number Flooring is a rounding process that finds the nearest integer lesser than or equal to the given number.

Check how flooring is different for positive and negative integers in the following sections.

Examples: 17.89 is rounded to the nearest flooring integer is 17.

-678.56 is rounded to the closest flooring integer is -679.

How to Round Number Using Flooring?

Have a look at the step by step process to round the numbers with flooring function easily.

  • At first, identify the given number.
  • If the number is positive, then we will round down.
  • If the number is negative, then we will round up.
