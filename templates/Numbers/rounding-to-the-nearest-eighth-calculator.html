Steps to Round to the Nearest Eighth

Go through the below sections to get the step by step process to round a number to the nearest multiple of 8.

  • Take any number to round it to the nearest eighth.
  • Get the multiple numbers of 8 that are nearest to the given number.
  • Find the difference between those numbers and the given number.
  • Round it to the number which has lesser difference.

Example

Question: What is 76.52 rounded to the nearest eighth?

Solution:

Given number is 76.52

The nearest multiples of 8 are 64, 72 and 80

Find the difference between the number and multiples

64 - 76.52 = -12.52

72 - 76.52 = -4.52

80 - 76.52 = 3.48

So, the rounding to the nearest 8th is 80.

FAQ's on Rounding to the Nearest Multiple of 8 Calculator

1. What is the rule for rounding when estimating?

Rounding is a kind of estimating. When you round a number to the nearest multiple of 8, get the multiples that are close to the given numbers. Based on those numbers difference, round up or down the numbers.

2. How do you round off number 8?

If a number that is rounding followed by 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 round the number up. If the number that is rounding followed by 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 round the number down.

3. What are the steps for rounding a number?

The first step is to circle the place value of the digit to be rounded. That is called the rounding digit. Check for the neighbouring digit on the right. If it is less than 4, keep the rounding digit the same. Otherwise, increase the rounding digit.
