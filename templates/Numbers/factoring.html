What is Factoring?

Factoring of a number or factorization of a number is the process of breaking numbers which when multiplied together results in the original number. The process of splitting the numbers into its factors is called factoring of numbers. Factors are whole numbers that when multiplied together produce another number. If a x b = c then a, b are the factors of c. When you think of factors in the division, factors of a number include the numbers that evenly divide the given number leaving no remainder.
