How to Round to the Nearest 5 Cents

Here we are going to discuss step by step procedure on how to round off to the nearest of 5 cents. By following the guidelines you can easily do it.

  • First, we should know the nearest cent value.
  • Then, if we have the value 0 or 5 simply do nothing.
  • If the value is 3, 4, 8 or 9 then round up to the nearest 5 cents or else round down to the nearest 5 cents.

Check the one-to-one mapping of nearest cents to nearest 5 cents below.

  • 0 → 0
  • 1 → 0
  • 2 → 0
  • 3 → 5
  • 4 → 5
  • 5 → 5
  • 6 → 5
  • 7 → 5
  • 8 → 0 increase the tenth place by 1
  • 9 → 0 increase the tenth place by 1
