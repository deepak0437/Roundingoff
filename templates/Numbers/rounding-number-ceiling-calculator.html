What does Rounding Number Ceiling Mean?

Rounding Number Ceiling is the process of finding the closest integer greater than or equal to given number. Check how ceiling is different for positive and integers in the later sections.

How to find Rounded Ceiling of a Number?

Follow the step by step process outlined below on how to round and find the ceiling of a given number. They are as such

  • Firstly, identify the given number.
  • Next step is to round the given number to its closest integer.
  • If the given number is a positive number we will round up ex: 953.67 nearest rounding ceiling number is 954
  • If the given number is a negative number we will round down ex: -453.34 nearest rounding ceiling number is -453
