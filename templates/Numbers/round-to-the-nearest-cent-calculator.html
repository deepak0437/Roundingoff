How to Round to the Nearest Cent?

Round to the nearest cent is nothing but rounding off to two decimal places. It has one basic rule, i.e. after decimal point if we have any number below 5 it is rounded down. And if that number is equal to or greater than 5 then increase the previous number by 1. If the number is lower than 5 then decrease the previous number by 1.

Steps to Round to the Nearest Cent

Follow the instructions which are showing below to round the nearest cent. By adhering to these guidelines you will get the answer correctly. Check out below steps.

  • First, to round the value to the nearest place, check for the thousandths place.
  • In this, what you have to do is if your value is greater than 5 you should increase the previous number by 1.
  • If the value is below 5 or less then decrease the previous number by 1.
  • That's simple, you will see the exact answer.
