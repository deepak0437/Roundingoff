How to Round Decimals to the Nearest Hundredths?

Go through the instructions listed below to round decimals to the nearest hundredths. Use them while solving problems on the concept and make your calculations simple

  • Firstly while rounding a decimal to the nearest hundredths place check the digit in the thousandths place.
  • If the thousandths place value is >=5 increase the digit in the hundredths place by 1 and remove the digits in thousandths place and thereafter.
  • If the thousandths place is <=4 simply remove the digits to the right of the hundredths.

Example

Question : Round 29.681 to the Nearest Hundredths?

Solution:

Given Decimal Value = 29.681

Analyze the thousandths value i.e. 1

As 1 < 5 the digit at the hundredths place remains the same and digits in thousandths place and thereafter becomes zero.

Thus 29.681 rounded to the nearest hundredths is 29.68
