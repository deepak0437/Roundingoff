What is Meant By Rounding Half Away From Zero?

Rounding half away from zero is a rounding technique that rounds the numbers further away from zero.

The rules for rounding half away from 0 are listed here:

If the decimal part of the number is 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, then round up the number.

If the decimal part of the number is 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, then round down the number.

Steps for Rounding Half Away From 0

Go through the following detailed step by step process to round off a number half away from zero easily. Follow these guidelines and instructions to obtain the rounded number.

  • At first, observe the given number.
  • When the decimal part of the number is equal to 5 or more, then round up the number.
  • If the decimal part of the number is less than 5, then round down the number.
  • Finally, rounded number is the number that is half away from zero.
