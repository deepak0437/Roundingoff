Steps to Kilometers Conversion Formula

A step was a Roman unit of length. A step is equal to two and a half Roman feet. One step is equal to 0.81 yards or 0.74 meters. Kilometer is the unit of length in the metric system. To convert steps into kilometers divide the number of steps by 1312.33595801.

Foot steps to kilometers conversion formula is km = Steps / 1312.33595801.

Process to Convert Steps to Kilometers

Avail the simple steps to convert the steps into kilometers.

  • Divide the number of steps by 1312.33595801.
  • The result is the kilometers.
