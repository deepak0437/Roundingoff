How to Round to the Nearest Thousand Step by Step?

Follow the simple steps listed below to round to the nearest thousand and they are as under

  • Whenever you have a number to be rounded to the nearest 1000 simply identify the thousands digit.
  • Next, check the number that is on right digit of the 1000's digit. If it is greater than or equal to 5 round down the thousands digit by one.
  • If the digit is on the right side of the 1000's digit is less than 5 simply round down and leave the 1000's digit same.

Round to the Nearest Thousand Examples

Example 1: Round 3892 to the Nearest Thousand?

Solution:

Given Number is 3892

As we explained in the procedure for rounding to the nearest thousand we will first identify the thousands digit i.e. 3

Next step is to look at the digit on the right of the thousands place i.e. 8

Since 8 > 5 we will round up and increase the thousands place by 1 i.e. 3+1 =4

Therefore, 3892 rounded to the nearest thousands place is 4000

Since 4 < 5 we will round down and keep the thousands place same.

Therefore, 42468 rounded to the nearest thousands place is 42000
