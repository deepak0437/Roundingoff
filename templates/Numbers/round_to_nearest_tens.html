What is meant by Rounding to the Nearest Tens?

Replacing the approximate number to the given number to the nearest 10 is called rounding off to the nearest tens. We have to follow a certain set of formula rules to round off the number to the nearest 10. So, below we have provided rules for round off to the nearest tens.

Rules to Follow While Rounding Number to the Nearest Tens

Rule 1: If the digit is one, two, three, or four then round down the number.
Rue 2: If the digit is five, six, seven, eight, nine then round up the number.

How to Round Off to Nearest Tens Without Calculator?

The best & easy process to round off the number to the nearest 10 without calc is explained here. Take a look at the steps listed below:

  • First, take the tens place digit and look at the right place value.
  • If the right place digit or one place digit is >= 5, then add 1 to the tens place, and the remaining digits after tens place become 0.
  • If the whole number place digit is <5, then round down the number and keep the tens place value the same.

Rounding Off Numbers to the Nearest Tens Examples

Example 1:

Solve 770 rounded to the nearest ten.

Solution:

Given number is 770

Here, 7 is in the tens place value and 0 is in the ones place value

The ones' place digit is less than 5 so round down the number for rounding off the number to the nearest 10.

Hence, 770 is the rounded value to the nearest 10.

Example 2:

Round 6787 to the nearest tens.

Solution:

Given number is 6787

To round the number to the nearest tens, consider the right place value to tens and check whether it is greater or less than 5.

Here, 7 is greater than 5 so round up the number by adding 1 to the tens place and the remaining digits become 0.

Hence, the 6787 rounded value to the nearest tens is 6790.
