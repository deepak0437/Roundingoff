What Does Meant by Round to Nearest Millimeter?

Rounding to the closest millimeter means finding the nearest simple value for the given number that makes easy while doing calculations.

Example: Rounding of 16.34 mm to the nearest millimeter is 16 mm.

Steps to Round off to the Nearest Millimeter

Go through the below sections to round off numbers to the nearest millimeter value. Follow these rules and steps to obtain the result.

  • Check the given number.
  • Find the tenths place digit in the given number.
  • If the digit is more than 5, then add 1 to the integer and remove decimal part.
  • If the digit is less than 5, then remove decimal part.
  • Write the obtained number as rounded to the nearest millimeter.

Question 1: Find 80.97 rounded to the nearest millimeter?

Solution:

Question 2: What is 7865.4 rounded to the nearest millimeter?

Solution:

Given number is 7865.4

The digit at tenths place is 4.

So, round down the number.

So, 7865.4 rounded to the nearest millimeter is 7865 MM.
