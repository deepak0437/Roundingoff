How to Round to the Nearest 25 Cents?

We are going to discuss step by step process to round off the nearest of 25 cents or multiple of 0.25. By adhering these guidelines round to the nearest value.

  • First and foremost thing we need to do is to check the two digits sequence xy of nearest cent value to the right of the decimal point.
  • If xy mod(25) <= 12 then we round down, and the nearest quarter will be xy - xy mod(25)
  • If xy mod(25) > 12 then we round up, and the nearest quarter will be xy + (25 – xy mod(25)).
  • If you round up and the nearest value is 00 then ones position will be increased by 1.
  • By the above formula we can say that if xy is 00, 25, 50 and 75 we will do nothing.

Question : Round the amounts $5.65 to the Nearest 25 Cents?

Given, value $5.65
