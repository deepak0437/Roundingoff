How to Round to the Nearest Hundred?

Here, we are providing you step by step process to round off the numbers to the nearest hundred.

  • Primary thing you need to do is mark the value in hundred place to round it off.
  • Then, we need to look for the tens place, that is available right to the hundred place.
  • If the rounding digit is 0,1,2,3,4, then change all the values in the right of the rounding digit to zeros.
  • If the rounding digit is 5,6,7,8,9, then add one to the rounding value and change all the digits after rounding value to zero.
  • Finally, you will see the desired number.
