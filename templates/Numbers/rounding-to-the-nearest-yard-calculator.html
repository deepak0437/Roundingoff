What Does Meant by Round to Nearest Yard?

Rounding of number to the closest yard means estimating the approximate value of it. This process makes your measurements or calculations simpler and easier.

Example: Rounding of 73.6 to the nearest Yard is 74 yd.

Steps to Round off to the Nearest Yard

The decimal numbers can be rounded to the nearest yard by using the simple guidelines and rules provided below. They are alsong the lines:

  • Firstly, get a number for rounding.
  • Check the tenths digit of the number.
  • If the tenths digit of decimal number is more than 5, then round up the number.
  • If the tenths digit of number is lesser than 5, then round down the number.
  • Write the number as rounded one.

Problems on Rounding Numbers to Nearest Yard

Question 1: Find 4982.4 to the nearest Yard?

Solution:

Given number is 4982.4

The digit at tenth place is 4 which is less than 5.

So, round down the number.

Therefore, 4982.4 rounded to the nearest Yard is 4982 yd.

Question 2: What is 595.7 rounded to the nearest Yard?

Solution:

Given number is 595.7

The digit at tenths place is 7.

The digit is more than 5

So, round up the number

Therefore, 595.7 rounded to the nearest Yard is 596 yd.
