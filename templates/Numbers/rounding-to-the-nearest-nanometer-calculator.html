What is Meant by Rounding to Nearest Nanometer?

Round off decimal numbers to the nearest nanometer means eliminating the decimal part of the given number and getting the approximate to it quickly.

Example: Rounding of 1605.3 to the nearest nanometer is 1605 nm.

Detailed Steps to Round off to the Nearest Nanometer

Have a look at the step by step process to round to the nearest nanometer easily.

  • At first, obtain the number.
  • Identify the rounding digit which at the tenths place.
  • When the rounding digit is lesser than 5, then round down the number.
  • When the rounding digit is greater than or equal to 5, then add 1 to number and trim digits after decimal point.
  • Write the remaining number as rounded to the nearest nanometer.

Solved Questions on Rounding Numbers to Nearest Nanometer

Question 1: What 634.9 to the nearest nanometer?

Solution:

Given number is 634.9

The digit at tenth place is 9 which is more than 5.

So, round up the number. Add 1 to the number.

Therefore, 634.9 rounded to the nearest nanometer is 635 nm.

Question 2: What is 12849.1 rounded to the nearest nanometer?

Solution:

Given number is 12849.1

The digit at tenths place is 1.

The digit is less than 5

So, round down the number

Therefore, 12849.1 rounded to the nearest nanometer is 12845 nm.

FAQs on Rounding to the Nearest Nanometer Calculator

1. How do you round to the nearest nanometer?

To round decimal numbers to the nearest nanometer, identify the digit at tenth place. If the digit is less than 5, then round down. Otherwise round up the number to get the answer.

2. What is 154.8 rounded to the nearest nm?

Number 154.8 rounded to the nearest nanometer is 155 nm.

3. What is 1 nanometer called?

The nanometer is the unit of length in the metric system. One nanometer is equal to one billionth of meter or 0.000000001 m. The symbol used to represent nanometer is nm.
