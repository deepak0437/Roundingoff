What are Percent and Permille?

In mathematics, per cent or more popularly written as ‘percent’ is a fraction of 100 expressed in the form of a ratio. It literally means ‘parts per hundred’ and is represented by the symbol ‘%’

Permille is a Latin word used in mathematics. Similar to percent, it is a fraction of 1000 expressed as a ratio. It means ‘parts per thousand’  the symbol ‘‰’represents it. Both percent & permille are unit-less entities, which have no unit for measurement.
