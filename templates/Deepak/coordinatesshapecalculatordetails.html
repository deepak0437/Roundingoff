1. How do you find coordinates in geometry?

To find out the coordinates of a point in the coordinate system you do the opposite. Begin at the point and follow a vertical line either up or down to the x-axis. There is your x-coordinate. And then do the same but following a horizontal line to find the y-coordinate.

2. What is the coordinate of a point?

A pair of numbers that describe the exact position of a point on a cartesian plane by using the horizontal and vertical lines called the coordinates. Usually represented by (x, y) the x value and y value of the point on a graph.
