What is Crore in Numeral System?

The numeral system is defined as a writing system to express numbers in a systematic manner. The most commonly used numeral system is the Hindu-Arabic numeral system. In the Indian numeral system, a crore is a number that comes after ten lakhs and it has 7 zeros. The Indian numeral system sequence is given here.

  • 1 = one
  • 10 = ten
  • 100 = hundred
  • 1000 = thousand
  • 10,000 = ten thousand
  • 1,00,000 = lakh
  • 10,00,000 = ten lakhs
  • 1,00,00,000 = crore
  • 10,00,00,000 = ten crores

How Many Zeros in 1 Crore?

There are 7 zeros present in 1 crore

We know that 1 crore = 100 lakhs

1 lakh = 1,00,000

So, 1 crore = 100 (1,00,000)

= 1,00,00,000

Thus, the number of zeros in a crore is 7
