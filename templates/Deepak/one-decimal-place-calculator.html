What does rounding to 1 decimal place mean?

Rounding to 1 Decimal Place is nothing but rounding to nearest tenths. Whether you will round up or down the number depends on the number in the hundredths place. 

How to Round to One Decimal Place?

Follow the simple steps over here to learn how to round off to one decimal place easily. They are along the lines

  • Firstly identify the given decimal number.
  • See if the hundredths digit is greater than or equal to 5 we will round up the tenths place by 1 and remove the digits on the right of it. If the hundredths place is 9 make it 0 and add 1 to the left of decimal place
  • If the hundredths digit is less than 4 round down and keep the digit in hundredths place as it is.
