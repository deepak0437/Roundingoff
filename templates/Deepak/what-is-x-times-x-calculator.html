Question 1: What is 2X times 2X?
Solution: 2X times 2X is equal to 4 X2

Step 1: From the equation 2X x 2X,

2 and 2 are coefficients and X and X are variables.

Step 2: When a coefficient is written with a variable, there is an invisible multiplication between coefficient and variable.

Step 3: Hence the above equation can be written as 2 x X x 2 x X

Step 4: Multiplying and coffeicents together give us

2 x 2 =4.

Step 5: Multiplying variables together gives us X x X = X2

Step 6: Combine the coefficient and variable answer together gives as 4X2, 

Hence the answer of 2X x 2X = 4X2

 Solution: 

The equation 5X times 3X can be written as 5X x 3X.
