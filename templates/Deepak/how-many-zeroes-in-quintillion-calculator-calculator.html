What is a Quintillion?

Quintillion is the denomination used for large numbers. A quintillion is a number for 10 raised to the power of 18 which is 1 followed by 18 zeros. In other words, a quintillion is a million million millions or thousand quadrillions.

  • 1 Quintillion = 1,000,000,000,000,000,000 = 1 x 1018
  • 1 Quintillion = 1 million million millions
  • 1 Quintillion = 1 billion billions
  • 1 Quintillion = 1 million trillions
  • 1 Quintillion = 1 thousand quadrillions
