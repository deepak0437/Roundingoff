What is Addition?

Addition is a method through which you can combine more than one number to get the total value of those numbers. Also, the total number that you get at the end is known as the sum, whereas the numbers that you initially add up to get the total value are called addends. This calculator will help you get the two addends for your sum.

How to Find Numbers that Add up to?

You can only find the two numbers that add up to your result by trial and error. But to make your process simpler, your addends can start from 1, and end either when both the addends are the same number, or they have a difference of 1.
