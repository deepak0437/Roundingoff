What is a Billion?

Billion is a 10 digit large number that is written as 1 followed by 9 zeros. It is also known as 1000 Millions. Billion can be denoted in two notations one is American System and the other is Non American System. In American System Billion is expressed as 109 where as in Non American System it is 1012.
