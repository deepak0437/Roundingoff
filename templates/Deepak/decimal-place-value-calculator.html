What is the Decimal Place Value?

A decimal number has 2 parts called the whole number and the decimal number part. The dot which separates both numbers is called a decimal point. The number which is available before the decimal point is called the whole number, and the number that is present after the decimal point is called the decimal number part. The place value indicates the position of the number.

FAQs on Decimal Place Value Calculator

1. How much is 2 decimal places?

2 decimal places mean 2 numbers that are present after the decimal point. Those decimal places are tenths and hundredths.

2. What is the 1 decimal place?

One decimal place to the left of the decimal point is the one place. The number to the right after the decimal point is the tenth place.

3. How to find the place value of decimals?

The first digit after the decimal point is the tenth place. The next digit is hundredths place and so on.

4. Why is decimal place value important?

The decimal place value is important as it provides the foundation for regrouping, multiple digit multiplication, and so on.
