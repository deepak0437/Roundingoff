Minutes of a Hour to Percent Conversion Formula

Every hour has 60 minutes and percent means per hundred. Based on this, the formula to convert minutes of a hour into percentages is as follows:

Percentage = (100 x Minutes) ÷ 60

Percentage = (5 x Minutes) ÷ 3

Steps to Convert Minutes of a Hour into %

The following is the detailed step by step process for representing the given minutes of a hour into a percentage.

  • Note down the minutes of an hour.
  • Multiply the minutes by 5.
  • Divide the product by 3 to check the percentage.
