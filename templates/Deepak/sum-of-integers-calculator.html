Sum of Integers Formula

The formula to calculate the sum of integers is here:

Sum = n/2 x (a + n)

Here,

n is the total number of terms

a is the first term

d is the common difference

Sum of first n natural numbers formula is sum = [n(n+1)]/2

Here,

n is the number of terms in the series

How to Calculate the Sum of Integers?

The detailed steps to compute the sum of integers are mentioned here:

  • Initially, write the integers in order.
  • Make a note of the first term, common difference, and the total number of terms.
  • Place the values in the formula.
  • The answer is the sum.

Example:

What is the sum of integers from 1 to 100?

Solution:

Given that,

Integers from 1 to 100 are 1, 2, 3, 4, . . 99, 100

The sum of the first n natural numbers formula is Sum = [n(n+1)]/2

So, n = 100

Sum of numbers from 1 to 100 = [100 (100 + 1)]/2

= [100 x 101]/2

= 50 x 101

= 5050

Therefore, the sum of integers from 1 to 100 is 5050.
