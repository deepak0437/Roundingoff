Cm³ to m³ Conversion Formula

The conversion factor for cm³ into m³ is here:

cubic meters = cubic centimeters ÷ 1,000,000

As we know that 1 cm = 100 m

So, 1 m = 1/100 cm

Hence, m³ = (1/100)³ cm³

= cm³/1,000,000

Steps to Convert Cubic Centimeters into Cubic Meters

The following are the simple steps that are helpful for converting cm³ into m³.

  • Consider any value of cm³.
  • Divide the given cubic centimeters by 1,000,000.
  • The result is in cubic meters.

Example:

Convert 25 cm³ to m³?

Solution:

Cm³ to m³ conversion formula is cubic meters = cubic centimeters ÷ 1,000,000

= 25 ÷ 1,000,000

= 0.000025

Therefore, 25 cm³ is 0.000025.
