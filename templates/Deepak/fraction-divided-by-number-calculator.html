How To Find Fractions Divided By Whole Number?

Follow the guidelines that are given below to calculate the fraction divided by the whole number easily and manually. 

  • Firstly, write down the fraction that was given in the problem.
  • To divide the fraction with a whole number, keep the numerator in the fraction.
  • Then, multiply the denominator with the whole number.
  • So that you will get the answer in fraction form and this can also be changed to decimal form.

Solved Example on Fraction Divided by Whole Number

Question: Is 3/4 divided by 6?

Solution:

Given fraction number is 3/4

Multiply the denominator with the whole number 6 

 = [3/4 ] / 6

 = 3/4 x 6

 = 3/24

Therefore, the 3/4 divided by 6 is 3/24.
