What is Second Power? 

Second Power means when two equal numbers are multiplied with each other to get the product. Three are various ways to show second power but the common one is X2. This power is commonly known as the exponent and the number on which it is applied is called the base such as X is the base and 2 is the exponent or power. 

The second power is also denoted by X². It can be used where the superscript is not available. Another way to denote second power is by using a multiplication sign in between the two numbers A x A.

FAQs on Second Power Calculator 

1. What is power in mathematics? 

Power defined the base numbers which are raised to the exponents. The power can be 2, 3, 4, etc. It shows the number of the times base number is multiplied. 

2. How do we denote the second power of the given numbers? 

We denote the powers by an. To denote the base number to the second power the n replaces the value 2 such as a2. 

3. What is 12 to the 2nd power? 

To find the 12 to the 2nd power we will multiply 12 two times by itself. Such as 12 x 12 = 144. Thus, 144 is the second power of 12. 

4. How do we calculate the second power? 

You can either calculate the second power using a second power calculator or by multiplying the number twice by itself.
