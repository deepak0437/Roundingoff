What is Third Power? | Third Power Formula

The third power is generally calculated when the number is multiplied thrice by itself. These are commonly known as cubes for the given numbers. Third Power can be expressed as a3. For example, the third power can be called “4 cubed” or “4 to the power of 3” or “43” which is similar to the 4 x 4 x 4 = 64.

The cube for the given number can be found when the number is multiplied three times by itself.

number3 = number x number x number

How to Calculate Third Power?

We can calculate the third power for the given numbers in the simple steps: 

  • Check the given numbers in negative and positive forms. 
  • Now, multiply the number thrice such as a x a x a = a3
  • Thus, the third power of the given number is calculated. 

NOTE: the negative numbers when calculated for the cubes give a negative product such as -a x -a x -a = -a3
