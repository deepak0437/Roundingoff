What is the Divisibility Rule for 12?

If a number is divisible by both 3 and 4, then the number is divisible by 12

Example: 2340 is divisible by 12

Sum of digits = 2 + 3 + 4 + 0 = 9 and it is divisible by 3

Last two digits = 40 which is divisible by 4

So, 2340 is divisible by 12

Guidelines To Test Divisibility of 12

Have a look at the manual steps to test if the number is divisible by 12 or not.

  • Firstly, take any number.
  • Now, add all the digits in the number together.
  • Check if the sum is divisible by 3.
  • Test if the last 2 digits of the number are divisible by 4.
  • If steps 3 and 4 are true, then the number is divisible by 12
