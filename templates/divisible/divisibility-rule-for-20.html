What is the Divisibility Rule of 20?

The divisibility rule of 20 states the number that satisfies the two conditions is divisible by 20. The first condition is like the remainder should be zero. The second condition is the number should have zero at the unit place and the tens digit must be even then it is divisible by 20.

FAQs on How to Find if a Number is Divisible by 20 or Not?

How is a number divisible by 20?

A number is exactly divisible by 20 if the last digit of the number is 0 and ten’s digit is even. The numbers 00, 20, 40, 60, 80, … are all divisible by 20.

What is another trick to check if the number is divisible by 20?

When the number is divisible by 4 and 5 then the number is divisible by 20.

Is 257 divisible by 20?

257 is not divisible by 20 because the last digit is 7 and is not 0, the tens place is having an odd number ie., 5.
