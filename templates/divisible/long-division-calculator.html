What is Long Division Method?

In mathematics, long division is a method for dividing large numbers into parts or steps, it means breaking the division problem into a sequence of easier steps. This method is a commonly used method used to solve division problems.

Dividend, divisor, quotient, and remainder are the parts of long division.

  • The dividend is the number that has to be divided.
  • The divisor is the number that divides the dividend.
  • The quotient is the result of division.
  • The remainder is the left after the division which cannot be divided further.

Steps For Long Division

To perform the division problem, we use the long division method. Here we are going to provide the steps to do that long division easily.

  • Take the first digit of the dividend from the left. Check if this digit is greater than or equal to the divisor.
  • Then divide it by the divisor and write the answer on top as the quotient.
  • Subtract the result from the digit and write down the difference.
  • Bring down the next digit of the dividend (if present).
  • Repeat the same process.
