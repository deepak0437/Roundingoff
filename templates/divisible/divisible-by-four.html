Divisibility Test of 4 | What is the Divisibility Rule of 4?

The divisibility test of 4 works like this, if the last two digits of the number are divisible by 4 or the zeros then the actual number is divisible by 4. Also, it is called the divisibility rule of 4.

For Example, let’s check with 124 is divisible by 4 or not. The last two digits of 124 are 24 and it can be divisible by 4. The quotient is 5 and the remainder is 0, so 124 is divisible by 4.

Solved Example on How To Tell if a Number is Divisible by 4?

Example 1:

Is 460 divisible by 4?

Solution:

Given number is 460

We know that the divisibility rule of 4 says the last two digits of a number are divisible by 4 then it is divisible by 4, so divide the last two digits by 4.

i.e., 460 means 60 divided by 4

After dividing 60 by 4, the quotient is 15 and the remainder is 0

So, 460 is divisible by 4.
