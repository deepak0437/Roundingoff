What two numbers have a product of 96 and a quotient of 4?

Solution:

Given data are

Products of two numbers = 96

Quotient of two numbers = 4

Let’s assume that two numbers are x and y

Now, take the two statements as equations ie.,

Equation 1: x.y = 96

Equation 2: x/y = 4

Here x = 4y

Now, substitute x value in the first equation ie., x.y = 96

4y.y = 96

5y = 96

y = 96/5

y = 4.898979 (rounded to nearest 6 digits)

Take the help of y value and substitute in the equation 1;

Ie., x.y = 96

x . 4.898979 = 96

x = 19.595918

Therefore, the two numbers "4.898979 and 19.595918" have a product of 96 and a quotient of 4.
