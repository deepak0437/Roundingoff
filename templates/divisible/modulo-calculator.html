What is Modulo?

In maths, Modulo is also known as Mod or Modulus. Modulo is nothing but the remainder value we get when two numbers are divided. The mathematical representation of Modulo Operation is given by a mod b where a and b are two numbers. For Example 8 Mod 4 = 0

When 8 is divided by 4 we get quotient 2 and remainder 0. Thus 8 Mod 4 is equal to 0.

How to Calculate Modulo or Modulus?

Calculating Modulo by hand isn’t a difficult task and you can follow the steps below to do it. They are as below

  • Firstly choose the initial number i.e. Dividend.
  • Then, pick the Divisor.
  • Divide one number by the other number i.e. Dividend/Divisor and the result obtained is the quotient. Consider it as an integer division and leave the fraction part.
  • Now multiply the divisor with the quotient.
  • Subtract this number from the dividend and the number you get is nothing but the modulo operation of given values.
