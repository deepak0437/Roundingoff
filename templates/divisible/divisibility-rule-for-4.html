What is the Divisibility Rule for 4?

As per the divisivility rule of 4, a number is divisible by 4 if it has the last two digits that are multiples of 4. You can learn this divisibility test of 4 and check if the given number is divisible by 4 or not without actually performing the division.

For example: Let us say 24. 24 is divisible by 4 as 24 is a multiple of 4.

How to Test Divisibility by 4?

Go through the simple process over here to check if a number is divisible by 4 or not. They are as follows

  • The first step is to look at the last two digits of the number.
  • If the last two digit numbers are zeros or a multiple of 4 then it is divisible by 4.
