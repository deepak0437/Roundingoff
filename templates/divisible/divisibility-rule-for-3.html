What is the Divisibility Rule for 3?

The divisivility rule of 3 states that if the sum of the digits of the given whole number is divisible by 3 then the whole number is divisible by 3. For small numbers, you can easily divide by 3 but for large numbers, as it is time taking process you can easily use the divisibility rule for 3.

As 19 is not divisible by 3 the number is not divisible by 3.
