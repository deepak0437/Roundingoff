What is the Divisibility Rule for 24?

The divisibility rule for 24 is a number that meets the condition of the divisibility rule of 3 and 8 both then the number is divisible by 24. If you need to check whether the number is divisible by 24 or not then check if it is divisible by 3 and 8.

Step-by-Step Procedure To Determine The Divisibility Test of 24

Instructions that are helpful in telling the number is divisible by 24 is listed below, please follow them carefully:

  • Take the given number and add all the digits to check for 3 divisibility.
  • Summation result must be divisible by 3, if yes then the original number is divisible by 24.
  • Later, consider the last three digits of the given number and check if it is three zeros or divisible by 8.
  • If it is divisible by 8 then the number is divisible by 24.
  • Note down that the number should be divisibility by both 3 and 8 then only it is divisibility by 24.

Solved Examples on Rule of Divisibility for 24

Example 1:

Is 45000 divisible by 24?

Solution:

Given input is 45000

To divisible by 24, check the divisibility by 3 and 8 both.

  1. i) Verify with 3 divisibility:

The sum of all digits should divisible by 3

Ie., 4+5+0+0+0 = 9

Divide 9 by 3, and we get 3.

So 45000 is divisible by 3.

  1. ii) Verify with 8 divisibility:

Consider the last three digits if they are zeros then it is divisible by 8 else check whether the last three digits are divisible by 8 or not.

Here, the last digits are zeros. So 45000 is divisible by 8.

Thus, 4500 is divisible by both 3 and 8. So it is divisible by 24.

Example 2:

Is 78234 divisible by 24?

Solution:

Given number is 78234

Check for 3: 7+8+2+3+4 = 24
24 is not divisible by 3

Check for 8: last three digits are 234

Divide 234 / 8 = 29.25

29.25 is not divisible by 8

Hence, 78234 is not divisible by 24.
