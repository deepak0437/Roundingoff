What is the Divisibility Rule of 16?

As per the Divisibility Test of 16, a number is said to be divisible by 16 if it has an even thousands digit and the last three digits formed are divisible by 16. In case a thousand digit is odd, the number formed by the last three digits plus 8 is divisible by 16 then the number is divisible by 16.

How to Determine if a Number is Divisible by 16?

Follow the step-by-step process to identify if the given number is divisible by 16 or not. They are along the lines

  • In the case of smaller numbers, you can check the divisibility of 16 by simply adding the last two digits to 4 times the rest. If the result is divisible by 16 then the number is divisible by 16.
  • If the number is large and if a thousand digits are even simply look at the last three digits formed and if they are divisible by 16 the given number is divisible by 16.
  • If the number is large and the thousand digit is odd add 8 to the last three digits formed and if the result is divisible by 16 given number is divisible by 16.

Solved Examples of Divisibility Rule for 16

Example 1.

Check if 133456 is divisible by 16 or not.

Solution:

Given number is 133456

Here the thousand digit is odd i.e. 3

As per the divisibility rule of 16, we will add the last three digits plus 8 = 456+8

= 464

464/16 = 29

As this result is divisible by 16 the whole number 133456 is divisible by 16.
