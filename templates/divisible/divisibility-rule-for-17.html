What is the Divisibility Rule of 17?

As per the divisivility rule of 17, a number is divisible by 17 if the last digit of the number when multiplied by 5 and subtracted from the rest is divisible by 17. For example, let us take 745. 74 - 5*5 = 74-25 = 49 49 is not divisible by 17. So 745 is not divisible by 17.

How to Determine if a Number is Divisible by 17?

Go through the simple process mentioned below to see if a Number is divisible by 17 or not. They are along the lines

  • Firstly, multiply the last digit of the given number by 5.
  • Then, subtract the result from the remaining digits.
  • If the result is divisible by 17 then the given number is also divisible by 17.

Since 73 is not divisible by 17 the entire number 985 is not divisible by 17.
