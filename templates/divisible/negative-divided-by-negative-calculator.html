What are Negative Numbers?

Negative Numbers are the Numbers that are less than zero and have negative symbols in front of them. For Example - 6, -9, and -12 are all Negative Numbers. In Order to Multiply or Divide Negative Numbers remember two basic rules i.e. If both the signs are the same the result is positive. If both the signs are different then the result is negative.

How to Divide Negative Numbers?

Follow the step-by-step process over here to divide negative numbers. They are as under

Step 1: Divide the numbers regularly as if they are normal numbers.

Step 2: The next step is to change the sign of numbers as per the dividing or multiplying numbers rules

  1. If the signs are the same the result will also be positive
  2. If the signs are different the result will be negative.

FAQs on Division of Negative Numbers

1. What is the rule for dividing a negative by a negative?

Dividing a negative number by a negative number gives a positive number.

2. What are the rules of negative numbers?

As per the rules of negative numbers

  • If two numbers have the same sign then the result is positive.
  • If two numbers have different signs then the result is negative.

3. Do two negatives make a positive?

If you have two negatives one turns over the other and they add up together and makes a positive.
